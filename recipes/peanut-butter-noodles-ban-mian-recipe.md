- *2 tablespoons* creamy peanut butter spread (1 1/4 ounces; 35 g), such as Skippy's
- *1 teaspoon* lard
- *1 tablespoon* (15 ml) garlic oil, see notes
- *1 teaspoon* dark soy sauce
- *1 teaspoon* toasted sesame oil
- *4 ounces* (113 g) fresh wide noodles, preferably thick, chewy wonton noodles such as Twin Marquis
- *2* scallions (1 ounce; 30 g), white and green parts, thinly sliced cross-wise

---

## Prepare the sauce
Melt [[peanut butter]] and [[lard]] in a microwave-safe bowl (or over simmering water) until smooth, stirring as needed.
Whisk in [[garlic oil]], [[dark soy sauce]], and [[toasted sesame oil]].

## Cook the noodles
Boil a medium pot of water. Cook [[noodles]] until just shy of al dente, about 5 minutes; they should be cooked through and pleasantly chewy.
Reserve 1/4 cup noodle cooking water, then drain.

## Toss and serve
Whisk 1 1/2 tablespoons noodle cooking water into the sauce (add more water 1 tablespoon at a time if the sauce is too thick).
Immediately toss [[noodles]] and [[scallions]] with the sauce. Serve right away.
